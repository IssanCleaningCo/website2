Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Miami and surrounding areas including Miami Beach, Coral Gables, Brickell, and Downtown Miami.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes, we are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. Our insurance covers any potential damages or accidents during service.

What cleaning supplies do you use?

We use professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment while ensuring exceptional cleaning results.

How do I schedule a cleaning?

You can schedule a cleaning by calling us at (305) 317-2735, filling out our contact form, or booking directly through our website.

Do you offer recurring cleaning services?

Yes, we offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly cleaning services with special rates for regular clients.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations to avoid any cancellation fees. We understand emergencies happen and handle each situation with care.
